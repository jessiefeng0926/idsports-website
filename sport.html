INNOVATION THAT ELEVATES EVERY RIDE

Our Sport Street Coilovers prioritize responsive handling with controlled everyday comfort. Built on a high-performance monotube platform, 36-way adjustable damping and full-height adjustability deliver consistent feedback and precise chassis control. Minimizing body roll and sharpening turn-in response, they boost confidence through back roads and dynamic street driving, while maintaining reliable performance for daily use.

Benefits of Sport Coil Over Systems

  • Sharp damping for razor-sharp cornering precision
  • Minimizes body roll for agile highway maneuvers
  • Balances firm responsiveness with great control
  • 36-way adjust for track-focused tuning customization
  • Tuned for canyon runs and spirited driving
  • Optimized stiffness to eliminate harsh bottoming

Adjustable Camber Top Mount

  • Delivers precise camber adjustment
  • More accurate than rubber mounts
  • Boosts steering feedback directly

Rubber Bushing Lower Bracket

  • Reduces noise and harsh vibration
  • Enhances overall ride comfort greatly
  • OE‑standard bushing for stable quality

Customized damping force

  • Firm sport damping force
  • Aggressive shim stack response
  • Precise high-speed control
