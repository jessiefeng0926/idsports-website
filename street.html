INNOVATION THAT ELEVATES EVERY RIDE

Our Street Spec Coil Overs blend daily comfort with sharp sporty handling. Featuring a rugged monotube design, 30-step adjustable rebound and independent height adjustment, they deliver stable damping and precise control. Reducing body roll and improving responsiveness, they enhance stability during daily commutes and spirited street driving while ensuring long-lasting reliability.

  • Balances plush comfort with stable chassis control

  • Tuned for city streets and casual driving
  • Preserves full travel to avoid harsh bottoming
