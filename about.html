CNRACING COMPANY INTRODUCTION

Brand Origin Story

Founded in early 2002, CNRACING stands for "CHINA RACING". Back then, many doubted the quality of Chinese-manufactured products and advised a name change. True to its mission of building a truly exceptional Chinese brand, CNRACING has worked tirelessly toward that goal ever since.

Our Vision

Deliver the ultimate driving pleasure.

Development Journey

  • Initial Stage: Focused on manufacturing racing parts, including SHORT SHIFTERS, PULLEYS, CAM GEARS, BLOW OFF VALVES, and CARBON FIBER components.
  • 2006: Began producing COIL OVERS (twin tube) and TURBO CHARGERS, partnered with domestic racing teams TNT, DKS, and earned ISO9001 certification.
  • 2010: Launched BALL BEARING TURBO production under the POWESPIRIT brand, which quickly became a market leader in quality as well as sales.
  • 2012: Developed MONO Tube products, entered the Australian market, and secured an exclusive OEM agent there.
  • 2015: Entered the Japanese market and established a local R&D branch. Full focus shifted to COIL OVERS, with engineers from Japan, Australia, domestic OEMs hired to upgrade design, manufacturing, and testing processes—launching the LR (LINESRACING) brand shortly after.
  • 2016: Obtained a patent for the exclusive high-flow digressive piston, releasing 2way and 3way COIL OVER products.
  • 2018: Partnered with Jiangsu University to conduct in-depth R&D on automotive suspension systems, magnetorheological shock absorbers, and full electromagnetic shock absorbers.
  • 2019: Signed an exclusive agency agreement for Ukraine, launching the IDSPORTS brand.
  • 2020: Extended cooperation with Jiangsu University to advance joint R&D initiatives.
  • 2023: Sponsored the domestic Lynk & Co Cup racing event.
  • 2026: Developing off-road vehicle shock absorbers, preparing to launch the North American branch (slated to open in 2026).

Unique Product Advantages

  • Full-Chain Integration: Manages the entire product lifecycle—from R&D, design to manufacturing, processing, testing. Patented processing and post-treatment technologies for the core cylinder core ensure superior performance, long service life.
  • Comprehensive Testing: Owns a complete set of testing equipment, including test benches, fatigue testers, oil viscosity meters, thermal shock chambers, vehicle vibration testers—all to guarantee product reliability.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Maintains close collaborations with university R&D institutions, racing teams, drivers, ensuring products are fully validated from design to real-world use.

Current Company Status

  • Product Range: Covers hundreds of vehicle models, with two brands (LR-LINESRACING, ID-IDSPORTS) and three core series (Street, Sport, Track). The Track series is further divided into Drift, Track Day, Drag Racing, Rally options to suit different user needs.
  • Partner & Sponsored Teams: Partners with, sponsors racing teams including DKS, DRS, ORS, TNT, 441B.
  • Sponsored Drivers: Supports top drivers including Wang Qi, Gao Shanbo, Li Fu, Han Jiahao.
  • Global Reach: Has exclusive agents in Japan, Australia, Malaysia, Russia, Ukraine, Poland, Kazakhstan. The new official website is under development, with the North American branch in preparation for launch.

Future Plan

Will further invest in the development of off-road, electric vehicle products, integrating electrified adjustable functions to provide better products for the market.
